❓ Quick Help

What is ZK Proof? Zero-Knowledge Proof — verify a claim (e.g., "income ≥ ₹10K") without seeing the actual data.
What is a DID? Decentralized Identifier — a blockchain-based digital identity.
What is IPFS? Distributed file storage where credentials are permanently stored.
Data Revealed: None? Yes! Zero-knowledge proofs ensure no personal data is ever seen by the verifier.
